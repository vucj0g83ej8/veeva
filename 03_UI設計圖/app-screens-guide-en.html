VeeVa Member System Screen Guide

Based on the original card-style wireframe layout, this guide places the actual customer app and admin app screenshots directly into the proposal for requirement alignment and presentation.

Customer App Flow

Users enter from campaign announcements, sign in, complete the Veeva form, then continue to review status, the member center, latest information, and coupon redemption features.

STEP 01

Campaign Announcements

Displays available campaigns. Users tap “Start Now” to enter the login and survey flow.

→
STEP 02

Member Login

Provides LINE login so users can quickly create a member identity.

→
STEP 03

Veeva Form Submission

The app embeds the Veeva Consent Form. After completion, users submit it for review.

STEP 04

Submission Complete

After submission, the Thank You screen and pending review status confirm that the process has been completed.

→
STEP 05

System Messages

The bell icon opens system messages, including form completion, coupon delivery, and new campaign notifications.

→
STEP 06

Member Center

Shows member profile details, qualification status, received coupons, and commonly used member functions.

STEP 07

Latest Information

Presents medical and campaign-related updates as list cards, with publishing managed from the admin portal.

→
STEP 08

Coupons

Lists available coupons, expiration dates, and redemption buttons, supporting multiple gift and reward types.

Admin App Screens

The admin portal is used to monitor survey performance, member review, campaign content, latest information, and coupon inventory.

ADMIN 01

Dashboard

Quickly view completed forms, pending reviews, approved members, coupon inventory, and list status distribution.

ADMIN 02

Member Management

Combines pending and approved member lists, allowing admins to switch tabs and approve members.

ADMIN 03

Campaign Management

Manages campaign names, status, campaign period, and notes, with support for adding new campaigns.

ADMIN 04

Coupon Management

Manages coupon types, inventory, issued count, redeemed count, expiration dates, listing status, and stock replenishment.

ADMIN 05

Latest Information Management

Manages update titles, publishing status, dates, and categories, with support for adding new information.
